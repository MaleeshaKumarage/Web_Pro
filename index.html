About Us

We are a pentacode. Undergraduates from Rajarata University of Sri Lanka, Faculty of Applied Sciences. Enthusiasm towards the Informaion technology applications to day today life has driven us to do this project. Our goal is to make life easier of the research groups who are dealing with water quality parametric data to recognize what is much suitable for drinking purposes.
